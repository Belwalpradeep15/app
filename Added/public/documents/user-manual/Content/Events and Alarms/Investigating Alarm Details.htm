Investigating Alarm Details

Alarms are accumulations of events that due to their severity, frequency, or other properties you identify are classified as alarms. Alarms are written to a database on the Panoptes server, and forwarded to currently connected browser clients. Panoptes can be used to view the details of an alarm and listen to audio for individual alarms live or retrospectively.

To investigate alarm details

  1. From the View menu, select Show alarm list. The Outstanding Alarms dialog appears.
  2. Click an alarm listed in the Outstanding Alarms dialog to display the Alarm dialog , which shows additional alarm details. You can also click an alarm flag directly on the geographical map display to launch the Alarm dialog. The Alarm dialog appears.
  3. Select from the following:
    • Acknowledge - click to display the Add Alarm Response dialog where you can type a comment that appears in the Alarm Details window. For details, see Acknowledging Outstanding Alarms.
    • Resolve Alarm - click to display the Add Alarm Response dialog and type a comment that's associated with the alarm and visible using the Admin > Outstanding Alarms menu.
    • Audio - click the Listen button to open the Audio Player dialog and play audio associated with the selected alarm.
