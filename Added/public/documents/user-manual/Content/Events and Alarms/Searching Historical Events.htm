Searching Historical Events and Alarms

Search historical events specific to a fibre line or a region along a fibre line. These two options are only available when you are viewing a specific fibre line, and when you select a specific region on that fibre line.

Helios categorizes a group of events as related based on velocity, time, and other factors.

Click and drag to define the spatial region to which the filter is applied. This will filter to only display events that occur in that portion of the fibre (spatial region). To clear this selection, click .

To view historical event details

  1. From the Events Menu, select Historical search. The Search Criteria dialog appears.
  2. Select the desired criteria with which to search and then click Search. A list of corresponding events appears.
Notes:

If your search returns more than 100 entries, a link labelled Download All Results appears in the bottom-right of the Search Results window. If you click this link, your browser performance may slow while the search results are refreshed.

If event tracking is enabled in your organization, related events that also match your search criteria are grouped beneath a node and a twisty () appears to the left of that node. Click the twisty to expand that group and see the individual events.

  1. Click Post to display the item(s) in the Recent Events dialog and plot them on the fibre display for further analysis.
    Providing your search retrieved 100 or fewer entries, click Post to main display in the main menu bar to display all of the items in the Recent Events dialog and plot them on the fibre display. These events remain on the main display regardless of settings you have configured to clear events until you select Events > Clear Events in the main display.

To search alarms

  1. From the Admin Menu, select Outstanding alarms. The Outstanding Alarms dialog appears.
  2. Click Search Alarms at the top of the Outstanding Alarms window. The Search Alarms dialog appears.
  3. Specify the search criteria and the sort order with which to display the results, and then click Submit.
