Events and Alarms

An event is detected when the signal anywhere along a fibre line falls outside of a defined threshold for a sustained period of time. The specific thresholds for detection of an event type are defined on the Helios unit. Examples of events might be a truck passing near a monitored perimeter, a person walking or a pipeline leak.

Events are classified by type and are used to generate Alarms if they exceed predefined limits in Panoptes. For example, the sound signature of a person walking differs from the sound signature of a vehicle passing.
