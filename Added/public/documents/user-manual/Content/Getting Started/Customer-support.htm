Report a Problem

If there is a problem with Panoptes, a bug has been found, or you simply want to ask a question, this menu option enables you to send an email directly to the Customer Support Team at Fotech Solutions.

To report an issue or ask a question

  1. Go to Main Menu > Report a problem… to launch your local email client or send an email to techsupport@fotechsolutions.com.
  2. Read the guidance and compose your email. Give a good description of the issue, and your contact details. The email template will automatically include some information about the Helios system and software. Please DO NOT remove this information as it can assist us in determination of the problem.
  3. In some instances, Fotech Solutions will need to see the system diagnostic information. To download and attach it to your email, refer to Download Diagnostic Logs or contact the System Operator.
