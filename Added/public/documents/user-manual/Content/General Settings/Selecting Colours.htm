Selecting Colours

Set colours by either clicking your cursor on the desired in the display pane, or by entering the 6-character hexadecimal RGB value of the desired colour.

To select colours

  1. In any box where values are specified, click the box.

The Colour Control dialog appears.

  1. Do either of the following:
    • Type a 6-character RGB hexadecimal value in the box.
    • Click the desired colour in the display pane (or drag to browse the colour that will be applied). Drag the slider to the right of the display pane up or down to change the hue.
  1. Press [TAB] or click off of the Colour Control dialog to apply the colour.
